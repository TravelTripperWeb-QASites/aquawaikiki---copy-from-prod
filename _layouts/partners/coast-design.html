Key West Triathlon & Expo: TRIKW Swim-Bike-Run in Paradise

View Details

DECEMBER 1ST TO 3RD, 2016 - 7AM

Event Details:

Swim in the Atlantic Ocean, bike along US1 and run along Smathers Beach. The Key West Triathlon and Expo expects to host a maximum of 1,000 participants and some of the sport's best known triathletes; TRIKW allows you to bask in the warm weather and the waters of the Atlantic Ocean while the rest of the country is experiencing winter. Olympic and Sprint distances.
